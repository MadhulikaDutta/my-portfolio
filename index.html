About Me

I am a data enthusiast with a strong background in data analytics, machine learning, and product management, holding an M.S. in Data Analytics from San Jose State University and an M.B.A. in General Management from IIT Kharagpur. With hands-on experience at leading companies like Walmart, Amazon, and Accenture, I have honed my skills in data processing, advanced modeling, and creating actionable insights that drive business decisions. My expertise spans analyzing large datasets using Python, PySpark, and SQL, developing recommender systems, and building interactive dashboards with tools like Tableau and PowerBI. I am passionate about translating complex data into strategic insights that optimize user experience and business growth. Whether it's collaborating with cross-functional teams or diving into deep technical challenges, I thrive in dynamic environments where data makes a difference. If you have any questions feel free to reach out!
